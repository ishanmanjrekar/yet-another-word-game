<h1>🎮 YAWG: Yet Another Word Game 🎮</h1>

<blockquote>Definitions meet the grid. Find the right letters, use power-ups when stuck, and see how many stages you can clear.</blockquote>

<h2>🏷️ Recommended Tags</h2>
<p><code>Word Game</code>, <code>Puzzle</code>, <code>Arcade</code>, <code>Casual</code>, <code>Minimalist</code>, <code>Educational</code>, <code>Browser-based</code>, <code>Singleplayer</code></p>

<h2>📝 Introduction</h2>

<p>I built <strong>YAWG</strong> (Yet Another Word Game) as an experiment in arcade-style puzzle design. The goal was to take the familiar "word search" format and flip it: instead of scanning for hidden words, you are given dictionary definitions and must construct the answers from a shared grid of tiles.</p>

<p>The design focuses on a "no-strikes" flow where players can experiment freely with letter combinations without being penalized for wrong guesses, as long as they beat the stage timer. It's built for quick, satisfying sessions with a steady difficulty curve that respects the player's vocabulary through CEFR-tiered word selection.</p>

<p>On the technical side, the project explores the speed of building a functional web game using <strong>Google Antigravity</strong> and <strong>Google Stitch</strong>. It features an offline-first lexicon and a system designed for remote balancing tweaks, allowing for adjustments to difficulty and economy without a full update.</p>

<h2>🕹️ Controls</h2>

<ul>
    <li><strong>Selection:</strong> Click, Tap, or use your <strong>Keyboard</strong> to pick letters from the grid.</li>
    <li><strong>Submission:</strong> The game automatically checks your word once you've filled the slots.</li>
    <li><strong>Toggle:</strong> Click the definition area (or use Tab) to switch between unsolved words in a stage.</li>
</ul>

<h2>✨ Features</h2>

<ul>
    <li><strong>Arcade Mode:</strong> Survive through endless stages of increasing difficulty.</li>
    <li><strong>Power-Ups:</strong>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Shuffle:</strong> Rearrange the unselected tiles when you're stuck.</li>
            <li><strong>Lightbulb:</strong> Get a tactical highlight of a correct letter.</li>
            <li><strong>Lightning Bolt:</strong> Instantly place the next correct letter in its slot.</li>
        </ul>
    </li>
    <li><strong>Word of the Day:</strong> (Coming Soon, maybe) A shared daily puzzle challenge for everyone worldwide.</li>
</ul>

<h2>📜 Rules / How to Play</h2>

<ul>
    <li><strong>The Hook:</strong> You are given dictionary definitions and a grid of tiles. Your job is to construct the target words using any letters from the grid.</li>
    <li><strong>Freeform Selection:</strong> Unlike Boggle, you can select tiles from anywhere on the grid in any order.</li>
    <li><strong>No "Strikes":</strong> There are no strike limits or "lives," allowing you to experiment with letters freely. Just keep an eye on the stage timer in Arcade mode!</li>
    <li><strong>CEFR-Inspired:</strong> Word selection is guided by CEFR difficulty tiers (A1 to B2) for a steady progression.</li>
</ul>
